## Key Insight
Overall Brand Alignment Score: **6.8 / 10**. The brand is **top-heavy** — genuinely excellent at the entrance (Hero: 9/10, ADR-47: 9/10) but diluted in the middle sections where visitors spend the most scrolling time. The core brand promise — "Where Symmetry Is Policy" — is **absent from visual design entirely**.
